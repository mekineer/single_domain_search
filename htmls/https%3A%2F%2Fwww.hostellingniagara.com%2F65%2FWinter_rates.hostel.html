Other Discounts

Upon presentation of a valid ISIC card, get 10% off on the non-member shared room & rate or $5 off on the non-member rate for a private room.*

Upon presentation of a valid MEC card (Mountain Equipment Co-op), get $2 off on the non-member shared room & rate or $5 off on the non-member rate for a private room.*
