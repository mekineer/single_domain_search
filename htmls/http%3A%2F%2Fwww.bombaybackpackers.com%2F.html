Welcome To Bombay Backpackers

Bombay Backpackers is a chain of Backpackers Hostels (BnB's) in 4 cities of India ( Mumbai, Goa, Matheran and Pune) and expanding, aiming at covering most of the major cities in the country. Vibrant themes, economical stays, comfortable beds, a relaxed atmosphere, blazing wifi, lots of socialising and local breakfasts are the highlights of Bombay Backpackers. Bombay Backpackers is comfortable fully air conditioned backpackers hostel, catering to all the travellers/Backpackers transiting or travelling to mumbai.

Bombay Backpackers Bkc

A vibrant backpackers hostel located near the commercial hub, BKC. Convenient for the backpackers as well as the corporate employees and visa aspirants, due to its proximity to the consulates.The hostel has four dorms with a total of 30 beds, each dorm is theme based on the transport system existing in Mumbai. The first Dorm based on the yellow and black taxis of Mumbai, another based on the BEST bus service, the third based on the Local train and the fourth based on the tuk-tuk’s. Local Indian breakfast is available each morning at the property; Bombay Backpackers features rooms with air conditioning in Mumbai . Common areas to meet and exchange notes with fellow travellers. Indoor Smoking Area Available. Good transport connectivity and a travel desk at the reception, city tours etc being organized through the Hostel. Most popular Bars and restaurants within walking distance. Good transport connectivity and the most helpful staff. Set 2.2 km from Phoenix Market City Mall, 3.3 km from Mumbai Domestic Terminal-1, 5 km from Pali Hill and 6 km from Juhu Beach.

A perfect transit hostel for travellers transiting Mumbai for a day or two or even for long term students or corporates. Airport is just stone throw away and is easily accessible at all times of the day. The train station is bang opposite the hostel, which makes your travel within the city or to colaba much more easier and quicker Located at Vile Parle West, 1.4 km from Mumbai Domestic Terminal 1, Airport Hostel by Bombay Backpackers in Mumbai features a shared lounge. Bombay Backpackers Beachside

The only hostel in Goa which is literally at the beach. Wake up to the sound of the waves and just run to the Beach. Located in North Goa at Ashvem beach , which is one of the best beaches in Goa due to its white sand and landscape. The property has beach view dorms and private rooms. If you are a beach bum and want to spend your summers lazing around on the beach and sipping on some beers, BeachSide by Bombay Backpackers is where you should be staying. Our hostel has mixed dorms as well as private rooms, and is called BeachSide because you can literally hop off your bed and jump into the sea. (Just wake up and take a dip in the sea). We have a common kitchen where free breakfast is provided every morning and Lunch & Dinner is available if you sign up for it. Although you are right at the beach but if you wish to check out some other beaches or markets in Goa, you can rent a bike/scooter from the hostel and explore Goa.

Bombay Backpackers Matheran

The only backpackers hostel located in the perfect spot at this eco- hill station. The property has private rooms and dorms. Welcome to an Ecofrindly Zone, where the mode of commuting is walking or hiring horses to travel within matheran and exploring different 28 Points.Matheran is a hill station located in Maharashtra (very close to Mumbai) on the Western Ghats range at an elevation of around 800 m (2,625 feet) above sea level. It is around 3 Hours train ride from Mumbai Central line, Kurla Railway Station. Matheran is well connected to Mumbai (100 km) & Pune (120 km) by rail and road, while Neral is the nearest rail station. The Matheran Light Railway (MLR) offers limited seats for each trip. You can even hire a local van to travel till the Dhindi Point. From Dhindi Point you have to walk for about 30 minutes along the railway track to reach Matheran. The only form of automobile allowed in Matheran is an ambulance operated by the Municipality. No private automobiles are allowed in Matheran. Within Matheran transport facilities available are horses and hand-pulled rickshaws.

Bombay Backpackers Pune

We’ve got great news for all you budget travellers! Bombay Backpackers have opened their first hostel in Pune city, in Koregaon Park A Spiritual abode amongst the foliate and lush avenues of the very Serene surroundings of Koregaon Park, within close proximity to the Osho International Meditation Resort and a lot of night life. Located within proximity if the Osho International Meditation Resort, the hostel features a lot of meditation/spiritual-themed decor and wall art. Their 4 dorms (with 6 beds in each) are named ‘Creativity’, ‘Love’, ‘Celebration’, and ‘Meditation’ – and are themed after Osho’s teachings. Each dorm has its own shared bathroom and is air-conditioned – except for the Meditation dorm, which is not air-conditioned. The two private rooms here are named ‘Poona' and ‘Antelope’ (after the town in Oregon), and each come with their own en-suite bathroom. In the spacious backyard of the hostel, ground floor, we’ve also introduced tent accommodation – weather permitting – with 3 private tents (for 2 people each) and one larger dorm-style shared tent. Breakfast is free, and the rates are super pocket-friendly. In the basement of the hostel, is a huge common area where you can relax and hang out with your dorm-mates. Board Games and other activities are on-hand as well. There’s also an attached kitchen and dining area, and you’re welcome to share the fridge and do some of your own cooking. Laundry service is available at an extra charge, Ironing services are free of charge, as are breakfasts! A private locker is provided to each person, where you can store your belongings while you’re out of the hostel.
