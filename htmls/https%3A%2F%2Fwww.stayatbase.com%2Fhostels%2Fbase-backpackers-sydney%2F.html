FAQ

Is there transport from the airport?

You have a few options:

Shuttle Bus: catch a shuttle bus from Domestic or International terminal, they will take you to the hostel for AU$15 one way. It takes approximately 20 minutes depending on the traffic, from 5am to 7pm, 7 days a week.

Train: Catch the train from either terminal - the Green line towards the city, get off at Town Hall station, Base Sydney Hostel is just behind Town Hall towards Darling Harbour. It costs AU$15.20 one way, they run from 4am to midnight during the week, from 5am on week-ends.

Taxi: A taxi costs $40-60.

Do you have Free Wifi at Base Sydney?

Of course we do! There's free wifi between 7am & 7pm every day, see the travel desk for the code. Premium wifi is available - $4 for 24 hours or $12 for a week.

Can I store my Luggage with you?

We have free luggage storage available for all customers staying at our Sydney Backpackers for 24 hours.

Is there a supermarket near to Base Sydney?

Woolworths is just a 2 minute walk away

Can I work at your hostel?

Yes, if you have a valid working visa and we have vacant positions! Check workingholiday.co for the latest jobs!

Do you allow Juggalo's to stay with you?

Yes we do, we let anyone over 18 with a valid passport stay. Your room mates might not like you in your Juggalo outfit though!

Do you have to stay at Base Sydney to visit the bar?

All Sydney backpackers are welcome at The Scary Canary. Come along and meet backpackers staying in hostels all over Sydney.
