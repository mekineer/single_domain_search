Backpackers House Venice is situated in Campo Santa Margherita area, one of the most beautiful and lively places in Venetian life, where you will find a many typical restaurants and cosy bars open until late. Backpackers House Venice propose simply furnished rooms and free Wi-Fi. A hotel's facilities include a shared lounge and a 24-hour front desk. Our Rooms, available with a private or a shared bathroom, come with a desk and fans.
The highlight of our hostel is the location: from Campo Santa Margherita you can reach in 5 minutes the train station as well as the bus station in Piazzale Roma. The main attractions of Venice, such as the Rialto Bridge, the Academy of Fine Arts, the Guggenheim Museum and Piazza San Marco are a short distance from the hostel too. Book your room at the hostel Backpackers House Venice and enjoy the wonders of Venice at a really low price!
