Just 5 minutes’ walk from Cardiff centre, Nomad Backpackers features a barbecue deck, games room and TV lounge. With 24-hour security and fair-trade breakfasts, there is also free Wi-Fi.

The compact private or dormitory rooms are bright and airy, with access to spacious modern bathrooms and free toiletries. Hairdryers, hair straighteners and ironing facilities are also available.

Guests have access to a fully-equipped kitchen, and there is also a decked garden with a barbecue. Nomad Cardiff also features a comfortable TV lounge with free movies, and the games room has a pool table and poker deck.

Situated in a Victorian terrace building, Nomad Backpackers Cardiff is just 10 minutes from Cardiff Queen Street Railway Station. The Millennium Stadium is just 20 minutes walk, and Cardiff Castle can be reached in 15 minutes.

This is our guests' favourite part of Cardiff, according to independent reviews.
