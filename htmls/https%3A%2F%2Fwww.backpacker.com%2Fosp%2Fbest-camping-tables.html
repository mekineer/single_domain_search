Best Camping Tables

Whether youre camping in the woods, picnicking in the park or tailgating at the game, youll find these portable camp tables easy to pack, easy to carry and incredibly handy.
Author:
Robert Prigge
Publish date:
Nov 13, 2019

Best for Family Camping

CampLand Aluminum Table-Height Adjustable Folding Table 

Sized Right, Packs Light. Ideal Instant Tabletop for Camping, Picnics and More. 

What We Liked:

Set-up is fast and easy with this table; the legs are easily adjustable and can be individually adjusted to adapt to uneven surfaces. The tabletop surface is approximately 36 x 20, which offers plenty of room for food prep, dining or a game of cards. When its time to pack up, the accordion-style design folds down to just over 7 wide and 5 high, so the table fits easily in your trunk, backseat or RV, and easily tucks away in a closet or on a shelf at home. It weighs just 10 lbs., yet has a 66-lb weight capacity to safely accommodate your portable grill or camp stove. It's available here.

Most Diverse

Lifetime 4428 Height Adjustable Craft Camping and Utility Folding Table

Camp. Craft. Work. Durable, Portable Utility for at Home or on the go.

What We Liked:

Aluminum tables are great, but their tabletop is less than ideal for some applications, including crafting and home projects. This durable Utility Table from Lifetime features a smooth, stain-resistant high-density polyethylene surface that is ideal for working with small parts and tools, and even makes for a great work desk for your computer and writing tasks. While heavier than an aluminum table it weighs around 20 lbs.its also more sturdy, with powder-coated stainless steel legs. The tabletop is a pleasing 48 x 24, offering plenty of room for food prep and dining while camping or tools and supplies for crafts and hobbies. Set up is easy, and the legs adjust to three height settings (24, 29 or 36). The legs do not adjust individually, so this table works best when deployed on a flat surface. Smart, simple design folds in half for slim storage and features a convenient carry handle for one-handed transport. Get it today.

Overall Favorite

Trekology Portable Camping Side Table with Aluminum Table Top  

Best Blend of Quality and Value. Highly Portable, Highly Reliable, All at a Great Price  

What We Liked:

If we had to choose just one camp table, it would be this one. Its lightweight and easy to carry, especially with the included carry bag. Its dependably sturdy when set up and is built to last. The legs feature oversized plastic feet, which add to reliable stability. They aren't adjustable, but the surface area of the table is just right. For an all-encompassing durable camping table, this one is your best bet. Get it today.
