HI-Niagara Falls Hostel

HI-Niagara Falls is a small and cosy hostel held by an international and friendly team ! During the summer, join us for a BBQ in the backyard or a trip to Crystal Beach. The hostel offers a bicycle rental service for you to discover the vineyards in the region or cycle down to the falls. Located just by the bus station, the hostel is at a 30-minute walk from the famous Niagara Falls.
