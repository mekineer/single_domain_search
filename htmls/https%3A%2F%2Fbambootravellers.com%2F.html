    WELCOME TO BAMBOO

    The gateway to the islands

    Fiji is a cluster of 333 tropical islands and is home to almost 1 million of the happiest, friendliest people on Earth!

    Bamboo is Beautiful Fiji’s fun, friendly and atmospheric budget traveller base. Located within a cluster of cool hostels in the midst of Nadi’s Backpacker Beach, it’s an ideal spot to stay to acclimatise, meet people, craft travel plans, and then launch out to some islands! Relax under a huge traditional Fijian thatch hut, swim and play down the sandy stretch of Wailoaloa Beach, swing in a hammock and enjoy the tropical surroundings.

    In a nutshell, BAMBOO offers:

    • Easy Airport pick up available 24/7*
    • Free WIFI
    • A location in the heart of Nadi’s Backpacker Beach that is on the bus route and just a 10 minute drive from Nadi International Airport
    • A Swimming Pool on the beach
    • A travel team for island trip planning with local promotions
    • A variety of clean and comfortable rooms
    • Delicious, cheap food at ‘Cafe BAMBOO’, featuring daily specials and traditional Fijian cuisine.
    • An island-style beach bar with happy hour and sunset cocktails
    • Live acoustic Fiji-style music every night
    • CCTV security
    • Key card access
    • ATM onsite

    Plus:

    • Currency exchange and Western Union onsite
    • Fun cheap Bamboo day trips to the ‘Sleeping Giant’, Natadola Beach and idyllic offshore islands
    • An adventure travel desk to plan trips further into the islands
    • Movies/Sports on a big flat screen
    • Free luggage storage
    • Kava parties!
    • Daily volleyball sessions
    • Table tennis challenges
    • Fire dancing shows

    The Bamboo Island Travel Desk can help to plan and reserve trips to beautiful spots at backpacker prices. Free transfers depart from BAMBOO daily for Awesome Adventures, Feejee Experience, Captain Cook Cruises and the Mamanuca Backpacker Boat. Local buses also stop outside throughout the day.

     

    * please email us your flight number in advance to secure an airport pickup plan

    WE ARE HERE

    Right on the beach! Next to international airport.

    It is approximately 10 to 15 minutes drive from the international airport. 
    Even though we are located right next to the runway, there is no direct way across it, so, the airport transfer will take you all the way around. 

    That's a nice trip itself. On the way to Bamboo our driver will give you brief introduction about things around. You will go through Namaka area with some government and commercial buildings, street shops, cafe and supermarkets. There is also a local market, where you can buy fresh local fruits and vegetables. Then you enter the area called Martintar, where most of local inexpensive restaurants and night clubs located. Driving through Martintar, you will finally reach the beginning of the runway and make a sharp turn towards the beach. The beach side area called Wailoaloa. From the turn to Wailoaloa, the remaining part of your airport transfer will be about 6 km long and will go through the beautiful green surroundings along the beach and the other side of the runway before you reach your destination.
