Collapsible Backpacking Table

A small company based in Toronto, Canada, Suluk 46 has been making thoughtfully engineered backcountry gear since 2008. Like the Tulimak table: a collapsible aluminum table for when you find yourself on wet or snow-covered terrain, or simply want a small platform to keep your kitchen setup off the ground.  It's super lightweight, assembles and disassembles quickly, and folds up thin to slip it into your backpack when not in use.

Note: At checkout, choose small (base price) or large (+ $6). 

Specs

Suluk 46 Small Table

  • Material: Aluminum
  • Dimensions, packed: 9.25 x 5 x 0.2 in (23.5 x 12.7 x 0.5 cm)
  • Dimensions, assembled: 9.25 x 5 x 1.5 in (23.5 x 12.7 x 4 cm)
  • Weight: 5.0 oz (142 g)

Suluk 46 Large Table

  • Material: Aluminum
  • Dimensions, packed: 10.75 x 6.5 x 0.2 in (27.3 x 16.5 x 0.5 cm)
  • Dimensions, assembled: 10.75 x 6.5 x 1.5 in (27.3 x 16.5 x 4 cm)
  • Weight: 7.5 oz (210 g)
