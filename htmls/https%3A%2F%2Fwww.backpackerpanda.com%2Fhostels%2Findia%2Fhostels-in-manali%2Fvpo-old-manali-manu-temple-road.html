Property Description

Checkin : 12:00Checkout : 11:00

YOLO Backpackers Hostel provides free WiFi, and guests can enjoy a restaurant, a garden and a bar.A continental breakfast is available daily at the bed and breakfast.YOLO Backpackers Hostel offers a terrace. Guests can make sightseeing and ticketing arrangements at the tour desk, or conduct business at the business centre.Hiking and skiing can be enjoyed nearby, while a ski equipment rental service and ski storage space are also available on site.Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Hidimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple and Circuit House. The nearest airport is Kullu–Manali Airport, 53 km from YOLO Backpackers Hostel.Old Manali is a great choice for travellers interested in nature walks, mountain walks and beautiful forests.
