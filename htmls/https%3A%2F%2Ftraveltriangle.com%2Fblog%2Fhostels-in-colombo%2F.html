    The capital city of Sri Lanka, Colombo, is also a buzzing commercial centre of the country. The city displays an amalgamation of its rich architectural heritage with colonial buildings and modern high-rise towers & shopping malls. This beautiful characteristic of the city attracts a lot of tourists every year. A lot of travelers prefer staying in decent hostels in Colombo than opting for luxury hotels firstly because they are economical and secondly because hostel experiences brings you closer to the city.

    Frequently Asked Questions About Hostels In Colombo

    Q. Which are the best hostels in Colombo?

    A. Staying in hostels will help you to travel around Colombo within your budget. The best hostels in Colombo are Bunkyard Hostels, Backpack Hostel 44, Colombo Downtown Monkey, Tuk Tuk Hostels, etc.

    Q. Why live in hostels while traveling?

    A. The cost of traveling is huge as you need to book hotels and transport to have a proper vacation. But, if you are a person who loves to travel at a regular gap, you can book these hostels which are budget-free.

    Q. Who can live at hostels in Colombo?

    A. Though everyone can accommodate in hostels, this accommodation is best for the backpackers. If you are traveling alone or with a group of friends, you can stay in these hostels.

    Q. What kind of facilities can you get from hostels in Colombo?

    A. Once you book hostels in Colombo, you get certain types of facilities to make your tour peaceful. In the maximum hostels, you can find an airport shuttle, free wi-fi, non-smoking rooms, free parking, etc.

    Q. How much do you need to spend at these hostels in Colombo?

    A. As these hostels are budget-friendly, you don’t need to get tensed about money. Usually, these hostels charge within Rs. 1500 per night.

    Q. How to book hostels in Colombo?

    A. If you are visiting at the peak season, it is always better to book early so that you get comfortable accommodation. Else, you can visit the city and book directly.

    Q. Can you live in these hostels with your family?

    A. There are several budget-hostels where you can live with your family. You will get a separate family room to book so that your privacy won’t get hampered.

    Q. Which is the peak time to visit in Colombo?

    A. To visit Colombo, you need to visit the city in December and January. You can also visit this city in September and November. So, book hostels accordingly.
