Airlie Beach Magnums Backpackers

Airlie Beach, Whitsunday Area

Accommodation

+5

Magnums is a tropical oasis literally in the heart of Airlie Beach. Relax in their beautiful settings or take the short stroll to the famous Airlie Beach Lagoon, Abel Point Marina or Port of Airlie.

Magnums is an "over 18 years only" budget holiday retreat in the middle of the beautiful Whitsundays. Magnums is within walking distance of all the fantastic restaurants, pubs, nightclubs and shopping precinct in Airlie Beach. Their affordable accommodation styles include dormitory rooms, cabins and private rooms which are surrounded by beautiful tropical gardens. Their rooms have air-conditioning and ceiling fans for your comfort. Professionally cleaned linen is provided and rooms are cleaned daily by their friendly in-house housekeeping team. They also have their Deluxe self-contained cabin, with kitchenette and ensuite.

Magnums' facilities include a huge fully equipped shared kitchen, undercover eating area, tropical outdoor eating area, rec and tech room with free internet, 51 inch plasma television, an outdoor gym (yes you read right!), coin operated washing machines in their laundry, free security deposit service and a free Wi-Fi area, all for your convenience. Magnums also has an on-site tour desk offering all day and overnight sailing tours in the Whitsundays and beyond.
