              Grab the finest motorized treadmills to make yourself fit and healthy

              Shashvat Vats| TOI Most Searched Products | Updated: Jan 29, 2020, 13:21 IST
              Looking for a suitable treadmill that can meet your workout regime and keep you in shape? Here is a list of some of the top-rated motorized treadmills available in the market that would keep you slim. Choose wisely.

              Are you one of those fitness enthusiasts who desire to trim their weight and burn non-essential calories & fats to be hale & hearty?

              If you are desperate to accomplish some serious shape and follow a regular cardio-centric exercise routine, getting a treadmill becomes necessary.
              Recommended By Colombia

              And since you and most of the other people do not have time to visit a gym/park/beach for a quick jog, getting a treadmill for home is not a bad idea.

              However, multiple choices available in the treadmill variant might confuse you in your purchase decision. For starters, there are two kinds of treadmills; motorized and non-motorized. So, to help you comprehend what's hot in this segment, we present our top picks for Motorized Treadmills available in India that you can choose from:

              Durafit Heavy Hike Motorized Treadmill

              Durafit, justifying its valor and reputation, presents the Duralift Heavy Hike motorized and foldable treadmill with a horsepower of 2.5 HP generated by a robust DC motor. In addition, the continuous duty horsepower can be clocked to up to 5.0 HP at peak output.

              This means that this motorized treadmill is ideal for running and jogging both.

              With a weight capacity of up to 120 kgs for walking purposes and up to 90 kgs for running, this motorized treadmill has a vertical hydraulic folding system, which makes it more convenient to fold and store.

              Also, if you a Digi-tech freak, this treadmill comes with hand-rail controls and a Bluetooth-powered FitShow mobile application that's compatible with both, Android and iOS, enabling you to manage the machine via your phone.

              Those who love to hit the cardio with their favorite music can do so with this motorized treadmill that comes with an Aux cable for connecting phones and iPads. Also, to ensure you are measuring your efforts in the right manner, this treadmill enables you to set & acquire targets on distance, speed, time, and calories via the target mode.

              This motorized treadmill comes with a 1.8 mm thick friction coated belt that offers you a running space of about 1270 X 470 mm.

              Powermax Fitness Tda-230 2Hp Motorized Treadmill

              This superior and first-string motorized treadmill comes with an auto inclination and a semi-automatic lubrication alternative to give you a unique running experience. Manufactured for light runners and joggers exclusively, this motorized treadmill is truly a home usage machine. PowerMax Fitness, being a chief player in the segment, offers this motorized treadmill that can bear a maximum of up to 115 kgs of weight.

              And if you want aesthetics and hi-tech features embedded in your treadmill, this product is ideal; with 12 distinctive console programs and a 5 inches wide rich blue LCD screen that exhibits time, speed, distance traveled, calories burned, and the pulse rate, this item is superb for fitness zealots who like to keep a check on their efforts along with output.

              You can also keep a regular check on your heart rate and BMI (body mass index) through a sensor fixed on the handle via a grip pulse sensor.

              What's more, is that you can curtail the stress on your knees through the Dual shock absorption and the ComfortTech 6-point Commercial Grade Damping System.

              With a speed bracket of 0.8 km/hr to 14 km/hr, this motorized treadmill that's made with 99% pure copper, comes with a multi-ply polyester strain weave running belt with a PVC and a run area of about 50 X 17 inches.

              Don't you wish a robustly built motorized treadmill with impeccable attributes that merge with your lifestyle and come handy in performing exercises in your own home? Of course, you do.

              WELCARE, an illustrious treadmill manufacturer, comes up with a MAXPRO PTM405 motorized treadmill that possesses a wide running space (1150 X 420 mm), a weight capacity of up to 110 kgs, inclination feature, 5-inch large screen, and long shelf life.

              You can get this treadmill that comes with a soft drop hydraulic system to easily fold and store the machine. Also, with this motorized treadmill, you can be sure of sustaining your target heart rate via the pulse rate monitor.

              To stimulate you with fun and inspiration simultaneously, this treadmill provides advanced features like heart rate monitor, calories burned checker, LCD display, time spent, distance traveled, USB port, MP3 speakers (in-built), Aux port, and cable.

              The run area stretches up to 395 X 1100 mm and the under deck of the machine comes up with 8 shock-absorbing rubber pads to shield you against sudden movements when running.
