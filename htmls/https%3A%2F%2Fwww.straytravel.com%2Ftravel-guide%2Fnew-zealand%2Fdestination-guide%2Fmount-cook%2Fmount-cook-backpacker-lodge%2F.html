Mount Cook National Park

Price

Mount Cook Backpacker Lodge is Stray's preferred accommodation provider in Mount Cook. Located right next to the spectacular Aoraki Mount Cook National Park, Mount Cook Backpacker Lodge offers a range of accommodation options and is the perfect hotspot to discover the Aoraki Mount Cook National Park.

Facilities:

  • Internet Kiosk or WiFi
  • Tour Booking Desk
  • Chamois Bar & Grill
  • Beer Garden & BBQ Area
  • Guest Laundry

 

Room Types:

  • Quad Dorm
  • Private Unit

Enjoy a meal at the Chamois Bar & Grill 

Located next to Aoraki/Mount Cook, you will enjoy spectacular alpine views
