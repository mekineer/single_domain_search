Appalachian Trail Backpacking

November 2, 2018 at 7:00 am - November 4, 2018 at 6:00 pm

| $151

The Appalachian Mountains are known as the oldest mountain range in the world and over the past two million years these mountains have truly developed into one of the world’s most unique and beautiful features. Join OP on a breathtaking journey as we backpack on a section of the Appalachian Trail and spend our nights under the stars amongst panoramic views and waterfalls.

Participant Guidelines   Register Now on Rec Connect
Trip Details
  • Current FSU Students: $151
  • Non-Students: $188
  • Departure: Fri Nov 2 at 7:00 am
  • Return: Sun Nov 4 at ~ 6:00 pm
  • Van Travel Time: 6 hours each way
Recommended Skills

This high difficulty level trip includes backpacking on the Appalachian Trail. Participants should expect about 16 miles of backpacking, which will be split into 3 days, about 2-8 miles per day.

Registration Fee Includes
  • Transportation to/from FSU Campus
  • 2 Outdoor Pursuits trip leaders
  • Camping Gear – tents, backpacks, and sleeping bags/pads
  • Stoves, Fuel, and Cooking Equipment
Participants Provide
  • Athletic clothing (for 3 days)
  • Hiking Shoes (boots or sneakers)
  • 2 Water bottles
  • Food: 2 Breakfasts, 3 Lunches, 2 Dinners, and Snacks
  • A bowl or tupperware to eat out of and a utensil
  • Hat, glasses, and sunscreen

All registered participants receive additional details via email from trip leaders prior to the trip.  Interested participants can contact the Outdoor Pursuits Staff with trip questions anytime!
