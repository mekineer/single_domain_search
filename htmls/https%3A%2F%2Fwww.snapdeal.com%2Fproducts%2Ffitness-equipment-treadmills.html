      How to Buy the Right Treadmill?

      Let’s see what points must be considered before buying a treadmill as every model comes with different features, design, and technology. After all, this is the best companion when you want to stay fit and healthy.

      Choosing the Right Motor and Horsepower for Treadmill

      The exercise bikes and treadmills that people place in their homes, offices or gyms have to be of updated machinery, high durability, good performance and a powerful, durable motor. The treadmill motor is one of the key features to look for while buying a new one. The Continuous Duty Rating needs to be considered as it is the measurement of minimum horsepower delivered while working or running on the machine. Also, it is a commercial standard applied to those used in health clubs and homes.

      As CHP motors are of highest quality, this ensures long run and smooth performance of the treadmill. 1.5 CHP is the minimum requirement with 4000 RPM to make the home users satisfied with the machine. One must shop for a treadmill that falls between the range of 1.5 CHP and 2.5 CHP.

      The treadmill is now equipped with a number of features that make the training session a lot easier. With the variable mode of incline and decline, you can also change the settings of speed to get the desired pace while running or jogging on the conveyor belt. You must select the machine that has pre-set and programmable sessions to have an effective workout. There are some models that can connect to a video player via mobile or internet to help kill boredom while working out.

      In addition, the adjustable mode of speed, LED screen featuring heart rate, calories burnt and pulse, and degrees of elevation are the key features to choose the best model. A treadmill helps to do exercise at all times of the day, from morning to evening, so there must a water bottle holder and phone rack to make the accessibility a fun-task.
