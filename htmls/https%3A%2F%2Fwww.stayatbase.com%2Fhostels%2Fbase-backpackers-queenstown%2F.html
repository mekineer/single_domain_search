FAQ

Is there transport from the airport?

Yes! The Airport Bus is the Number 11. It leaves every 15 minutes from the Airport and also from outside McDonalds in town. The drop off point is on Camp Street which is a 5 minute walk to Base Queenstown Backpackers. From Camp Street turn left at the main roundabout onto Shotover street. Walk straight down Shotover Street until you see our Queenstown Hostel & Loco Bar on the right hand side.

Is there free Wifi?

Absolutely! It's free 24/7 on the ground floor of Base Backpackers Queenstown, or for access from other parts of the hostel it's $4 day or $12 Week

Is there free parking in Queenstown?

The nearest paid and free parking to Base Hostel Queenstown is on Mann Street which is only a 2 minute walk away.

Do you provide free breakfast at Base Queenstown backpackers?

Between 8.30 and 9.30 am you can have toast and spreads, there's also free tea and coffee throughout the day.

Do you have luggage storage?

Of course, we have a luggage storage room available to all guests of our Base Hostel Queenstown. Shared dorms have lockable luggage lockers (bring a padlock though)

Is there ski storage at Base Queenstown Backpackers?

Yes, we have ski / snowboard storage available for all you snow lovers.

Do staff give free hugs?

Whenever you need one, the staff at our Queenstown hostel will give you one.
