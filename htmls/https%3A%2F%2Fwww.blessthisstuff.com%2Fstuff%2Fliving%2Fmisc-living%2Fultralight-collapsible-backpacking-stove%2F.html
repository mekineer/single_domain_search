When backpacking, packing light is essential, this Collapsible Camp Stove is the perfect cooking solution for when you venture out into the wild. The lightweight cooker is made from stainless steel and can be assembled in a matter of seconds to burn either wood or alcohol, the clever firebox doubles as a windscreen. After use simply dismantle it and nest it together to take up virtually zero space in your backpack.
